FROM EMOTIONAL NUMBNESS TO A THRIVING NEWFOUND CONSCIOUSNESS

Our emotions are buried deep in the ground, and well-rooted in the psyche. We must connect with ourselves, and unsurface our emotions to flush out the internal wounds. This shifts us to realize that the time taken to confront these wounds will no longer have the same impact as before. The release of old wounds feel lighter, freer and less restrictive. Breaking through the barrier between the unconscious and conscious, we enter a threshold of a deeper awakening. We gain a sense of clarity when we thrive above the ground towards self reclamation and self reconciliation.
